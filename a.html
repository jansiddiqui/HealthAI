HealthTips

Influenza (Flu)

Symptoms: Fever, cough, sore throat, body aches.

Prevention: Vaccination, hand washing, avoiding sick people.

Consult Flu Specialist

Diabetes

Symptoms: Increased thirst, fatigue, frequent urination.

Prevention: Healthy diet, exercise, monitor sugar levels.

Consult Diabetes Expert

Hypertension

Symptoms: Headaches, dizziness, blurred vision.

Prevention: Low-salt diet, regular exercise, stress management.

Consult Cardiologist

Asthma

Symptoms: Wheezing, shortness of breath, tight chest.

Prevention: Avoid allergens, use inhalers, stay active.

Consult Pulmonologist

COVID-19

Symptoms: Fever, cough, loss of taste/smell.

Prevention: Get vaccinated, wear masks, social distancing.

Consult COVID Specialist
